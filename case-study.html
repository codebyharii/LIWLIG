Corporate Event

Equinor: Celebrating
50 Years of Energy

Client Equinor
Year 2024
Location Stavanger, Norway
Guests 2,500+
The Challenge

Honoring a legacy while looking to the future

Equinor approached LIWLIG to create a landmark celebration marking their 50th anniversary. The challenge was to craft an event that honoured five decades of pioneering energy innovation while positioning the company for its next chapter in the energy transition.

The event needed to resonate with over 2,500 attendees — from long-serving employees and retirees to partners, government officials, and the global energy community.

The Solution

A multi-sensory journey through time

We designed a cinematic experience that took guests on an immersive journey — from Equinor's founding in the early days of Norwegian oil exploration through to their vision for a sustainable energy future.

The event featured a custom-built stage environment with 360° projection mapping, live orchestral performances, and interactive digital installations that allowed guests to explore the company's milestones through touch, sound, and motion.

The Execution

Flawless delivery at scale

Our team of 85 professionals managed every detail — from custom stage construction and AV engineering to catering logistics and VIP protocol. The event ran across three days, with each day themed around a different era of Equinor's history.
